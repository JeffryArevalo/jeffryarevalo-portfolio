## Overview

A complete business website for JL Interior Upholstery LLC, a furniture reupholstery and restoration company serving residential and commercial clients. The site is built for accessibility and ease of use, with bilingual support in English and Spanish to reflect the business's customer base.

## Features

**Service Showcase** — Organized presentation of all service categories: sofa and armchair reupholstery, dining chairs, ottomans, headboards, restaurant booth seating, and office furniture restoration.

**Project Portfolio** — A gallery of completed work that lets prospective customers evaluate quality before reaching out.

**Estimate Request** — A straightforward contact flow that captures enough detail for the business to respond with an accurate quote.

**Bilingual Support** — Full English and Spanish content so the site serves both audiences without requiring a separate URL or version.

## Technical Details

Built as a lightweight static site with no framework dependencies, keeping load times fast and maintenance simple for a small business owner. Mobile-first layout ensures the site works well for customers browsing on their phones.
